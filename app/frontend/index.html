Results ready in approximately 15 to 30 seconds

What to include

Demographics

Age, sex, relevant occupational or social history, ethnicity where clinically relevant

Presentation

Chief complaint, onset and duration, character and severity, associated symptoms, alleviating or aggravating factors

Vitals

Blood pressure, heart rate, respiratory rate, SpO2, temperature, GCS if neurological involvement

Background

Past medical history, current medications, allergies, recent procedures or hospitalizations, family history

Example input

"58-year-old female with sudden onset severe headache rated 10/10, associated neck stiffness and vomiting. BP 178/102, HR 88, temp 37.8C. No prior headache history. No current medications."

Output is generated by a large language model and validated against NLM terminology services. ICD-10 codes are verified where possible. This tool does not access patient records and does not store identifying information.
